Scrambled Eggs

Eggs beaten then cooked. A typical breakfast.

Ingredients:

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1/4 cup half-n-half
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter

Steps:

  1. Whisk eggs, half-n-half and salt together
  2. Melt butter in a pan on medium heat
  3. Add eggs to center of pan
  4. Remove eggs when cooked
