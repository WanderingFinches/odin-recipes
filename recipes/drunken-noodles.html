Drunken Noodles

A spicy, sweet, and savory bowl of thai noodles. It can be mixed with a variety of veggies, meat, or tofu.

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces dried rice noodles
  • 1/4 cup oyster sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablesppon Asian fish sauce
  • 1 table spoon maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 tablespoon cold water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 cup shallots, thinly sliced
  • 6 teaspoons chili padi, thinly sliced

Steps:

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Soak rice noodles
  3. Combine sauces
  4. Heat oils
  5. Add broccoli stems
  6. Add sauce mix with onions, simmer
  7. Add thai basil leaves
