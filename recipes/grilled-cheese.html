Grilled Cheese

A tasty treat of toasted bread and melted cheese. A classic!

Ingredients:

  • Bread of your choice
  • Cheese or your choice
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Butter both sides of bread
  2. Place two slices of bread on skillet, butter side down
  3. Stack cheese on one piece of toast, close sandwich when breads are golden brown
  4. Once cheese has melted, remove sandwich and cut in half diagonally
